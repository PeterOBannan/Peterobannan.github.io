Advanced Recovery Coalition (ARC)

Neutralizing Hazards. Securing Resources. Transforming Infrastructure.

ARC is a 501(c)(3) initiative dedicated to thermal extraction technologies in WTE infrastructure, converting hazardous ash into safe resources while harvesting critical rare-earth metals.

The SpARC Project: Spokane Pilot

Project Sp-ARC (Spokane Advanced Recovery) focuses on igniting the use of waste-to-energy facilities as closed-loop resource generators.

  • Biological Destruction: 3,000°C Flash Joule Heating destroys dioxins and PFAS.
  • Resource Recovery: Electrothermal Chlorination extracts defense-grade metals.
