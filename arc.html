Advanced Recovery Coalition (ARC)

A 501(c)(3) Scientific Corporation | EIN: 41-5214025

The ARC mission is to pioneer the integration of high-temperature electrothermal processing into municipal infrastructure. By solving the waste crisis at its molecular root, we protect our communities and secure our national future.

The Science of Remediation

Our process uses Flash Joule Heating (FJH) and Electrothermal Chlorination (ETC) to superheat toxic ash to 3,000°C in milliseconds. This thermal pulse obliterates dioxins and PFAS while vaporizing critical rare-earth metals for selective capture.

The Sp-ARC Plan: Spokane Pilot

The Sp-ARC roadmap involves building a modular, 1-ton-per-day pilot facility in the West Plains. This facility requires zero municipal capital and serves as the laboratory for national landfill mining and resource recovery scaling.
