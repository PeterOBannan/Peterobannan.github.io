The Sp-ARC Initiative

Project Sp-ARC (Spokane Advanced Resource Coalition) utilizes Flash Joule Heating (FJH) to superheat municipal Waste-to-Energy (WTE) ash to 3,000°C in milliseconds. This process obliterates organic toxins like PFAS and dioxins while vaporizing critical minerals for recovery.
