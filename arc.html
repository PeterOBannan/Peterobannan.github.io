Advanced Recovery Coalition (ARC)

A 501(c)(3) Scientific Corporation | EIN: 41-5214025 [cite: 284, 307]

The ARC mission is to pioneer the integration of high-temperature electrothermal processing into municipal infrastructure[cite: 241, 285]. By solving the waste crisis at its molecular root, we protect our communities and secure our national future[cite: 248, 250].

The Science of Remediation

Our process uses **Flash Joule Heating (FJH)** and **Electrothermal Chlorination (ETC)** to superheat toxic ash to **3,000°C** in milliseconds[cite: 246, 247]. This thermal pulse obliterates dioxins and PFAS while vaporizing critical rare-earth metals for selective capture[cite: 248, 288].

The Sp-ARC Plan: Spokane Pilot

The **Sp-ARC** roadmap involves building a modular, 1-ton-per-day pilot facility in the West Plains[cite: 259, 260]. This facility requires zero municipal capital and serves as the laboratory for national landfill mining and resource recovery scaling[cite: 260, 264].
