Advanced Recovery Coalition (ARC)

Neutralizing Hazards. Securing Resources. Transforming Infrastructure.

ARC is a 501(c)(3) scientific organization dedicated to the remediation of Waste-to-Energy (WTE) ash. Our flagship project, Sp-ARC, represents a localized commitment to the Spokane region, providing a zero-capital solution to municipal liabilities through breakthrough thermal processing.

The Science: Flash Joule Heating (FJH)

Using Electrothermal Chlorination (ETC), we superheat toxic ash to 3,000°C in milliseconds. This doesn't just "store" waste; it vaporizes toxins (PFAS/Dioxins) and allows for the capture of Rare Earth Elements (REEs) for national defense supply chains. The remaining material is a clean, "green" concrete additive.

The Spokane Plan (Sp-ARC)

Our roadmap moves from regulatory discovery with the WA Dept of Ecology to the construction of a modular West Plains Pilot Facility. By leveraging local WTE output, we turn a $10M+ annual liability into a critical mineral asset for the state.
