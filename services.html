Services

I help motivated people finish degrees faster, upskill with modern tools, and translate real-world experience into academic credit and leadership opportunities. My specialty is business administration and management (bachelor’s or master’s tracks), and I also support learners headed into healthcare, education, and other fields with practical “tool-building” and study systems.

Whether you’re a high-performing “bookish” learner ready to accelerate, or a builder from the trades with deep experience but less interest in traditional academics—I’ll meet you where you are and help you move quickly and confidently.

What I Offer

1) Degree Acceleration & Coaching (Business & Management focus)

  • Custom roadmap for your bachelor’s or master’s in business/management.
  • Time-boxing, milestone planning, and weekly accountability.
  • Competency-based strategies to finish faster without sacrificing quality.
  • Credit-by-exam / prior-learning pathways where appropriate.

2) Writing & Research Co-Pilot

  • From blank page to polished submission: outlining, drafting, revision loops.
  • Support for literature scans, notetaking, citations, and academic integrity.
  • Plain-language coaching for non-“bookish” learners; clear rubrics and checklists.

3) AI-Enabled Study Systems

  • Set up practical tools (task capture, templates, research pipelines, citation managers).
  • Responsible AI workflows to brainstorm, organize, and accelerate output.
  • Reusable prompts for reading notes, summaries, outlines, and draft refinement.

4) Prior-Learning Mapping & Portfolio Build

  • Translate construction/trades, healthcare, education, or small-business experience into academic outcomes.
  • Evidence collection: logs, artifacts, supervisor statements, certifications.
  • Portfolio assembly that aligns directly to course or program competencies.

5) Career Counseling & Professional Materials

  • Role targeting (analyst, operations, management, healthcare admin, ed leadership, etc.).
  • Resume/CV, LinkedIn, and statement drafts aligned to your next step.
  • Interview prep and practical leadership frameworks for new managers.

6) Application, Transfer, & Planning Support

  • Shortlist programs that fit your budget, timeline, and learning style.
  • Transfer planning: sequencing courses to minimize cost and time.
  • Scholarship/aid strategy and documentation checklists.

Who I Work With

  • High performers (HS or bachelor’s grads) aiming to finish a program at a hyper-accelerated pace.
  • Experience-rich professionals (construction/trades, healthcare, education, small business) who prefer practical tools over academic jargon.
  • Career shifters seeking a credible credential plus leadership skill-building.
  • First-gen and return-to-school adults who want clear steps, structure, and encouragement.

How It Works

  1. Quick consult: we clarify your goal, constraints, and timeline.
  2. 90-day plan: a concrete roadmap with weekly deliverables and tool setup.
  3. Execution & feedback: tutoring, writing support, and check-ins to keep you moving.

I prioritize low-cost, accessible routes and practical skill demonstration. If you’re not “bookish,” no problem— we’ll harness your lived experience to meet academic expectations efficiently and honestly.
