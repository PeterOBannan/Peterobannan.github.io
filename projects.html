Active Initiatives

Applying strategic leadership to complex, real-world infrastructure and institutional challenges.

1. The WTE & Advanced Materials Coalition

Focus: Environmental Remediation, Domestic Supply Chains, & Municipal Infrastructure.

Municipal Waste-to-Energy (WTE) facilities provide critical landfill diversion, but they generate highly toxic ash that is currently transported out of state at great expense. I am spearheading a coalition to integrate Flash Joule Heating (FJH) and Electrothermal Chlorination technology into the municipal grid in Spokane.

This high-temperature (3,000°C) process completely obliterates toxic organic compounds while separating and extracting highly valuable heavy and rare-earth metals. By bridging the gap between local municipal logistics, deep-tech startups, and federal defense funding, this initiative aims to turn a local waste crisis into a domestic asset.

2. Institutional Accountability & Reform

Focus: Transparency, Whistleblower Protections, & Healthcare Operations.

Large bureaucratic systems often default to protecting themselves rather than their personnel or constituents. Drawing on my experience at Eastern State Hospital, I am actively engaged in advocating for institutional transparency, dismantling toxic administrative cultures, and utilizing public records litigation to enforce liability within state-run healthcare systems.
