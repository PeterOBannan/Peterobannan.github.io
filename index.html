About

I’ve spent my career moving between education, healthcare, and operations — places where clarity, structure, and strong leadership matter most. I grew up in Spokane, and now my family and I are based in Medical Lake. My wife Tawnee and I value raising our kids with community around us — from baseball and volleyball games to our regular involvement at church. Those rhythms of family, faith, and small-town life anchor me just as much as my professional work in education and leadership. This site is a place where I share both: the practical skills I bring to organizations and the personal context that shapes how I lead, teach, and serve.
