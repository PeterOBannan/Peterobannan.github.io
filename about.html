Origins & Philosophy

I was raised in a large family of nine children, spending my early years in the North Central area of Spokane before moving to our permanent home shortly after starting second grade. Growing up in a massive, dynamic household built an early, instinctual understanding of systems, continuous variables, and the necessity of structural organization.

My professional philosophy is rooted in Radical Authenticity. I prioritize objective truth and operational efficiency over bureaucratic comfort. This approach has guided my career across diverse sectors—from hands-on construction and biological sciences to specialized mental health care and high-level strategic logistics.
